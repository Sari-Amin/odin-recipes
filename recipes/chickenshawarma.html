Chicken Shawarma

Description

This chicken shawarma is made with flavorful chicken thighs marinated in a spiced yogurt. Slice the cooked chicken and serve in pita bread with tomato, sliced onion, and my homemade tahini sauce for a popular Middle Eastern sandwich.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup malt vinegar
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon mixed spice
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground cardamom
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup tahini
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 medium tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup sliced onion
  • 4 cups shredded lettuce
  • 8 pita bread rounds

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Ingredients for chicken shawarma, including chicken, pita bread, lettuce, tomato, yogurt, spices, and sauces, arranged on a surface To make the marinade: Combine malt vinegar, 1/4 cup yogurt, vegetable oil, mixed spice, cardamom, salt, and pepper together in a glass baking dish.
  3. Marinade for chicken shawarma in a clear dish Add chicken thighs to the mixture and turn to coat. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Chicken thighs marinating in a glass dish Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). To make the tahini sauce: Combine tahini, 1/4 cup yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and parsley together in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper, taste, and adjust flavors if desired. Cover and refrigerate.
  5. A bowl of creamy shawarma sauce with herbs, placed on a light surface Cover the chicken and bake in the marinade for 30 minutes, turning once. Uncover, and cook for an additional 5 to 10 minutes, or until chicken is browned and cooked through.
  6. Glass baking dish with cooked chicken pieces in juices Remove chicken from the dish, and cut into slices.
  7. Place sliced chicken, tomato, onion, and lettuce onto pita breads. Roll up, and top with tahini sauce.
